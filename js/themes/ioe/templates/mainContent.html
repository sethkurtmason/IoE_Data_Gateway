Empowering Montana's Researchers

The IoE Data Gateway simplifies the task of discovering work produced by others and makes it easy to archive, publish and share your own research findings with peers or the international scientific community. Leverage the IoE Data Getway to increase the impact of your research.

Archive environmental data.

Store environmental and ecological data sets using the IoE Data Gateway. Meet grant agency requirements for long-term data preservation. Archived data is mirrored on mulitple servers at Montana State University and securely copied across the DataONE cyberinfrastructure network.

Publish and share with confidence.

Easily upload data to the IoE Data Gateway online, or use customized desktop software or clinet libraries built in Python, Java, and R to describe, manage, and share your data. Use DOI tagging to permanently publish and recieve credit for your data sets.

Execute powerful searches.

Quickly search archived research collections to locate data for your next research effort or connect with potential collaborators. Look for data stored in the IoE Data Gateway and other DataONE member node repositories based on keyword, taxon, location, date of publication, or author.
